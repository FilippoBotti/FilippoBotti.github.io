Who Am I?
I am a PhD student in Information Technology at the University of Parma. Currently, I focus my research on generative models, image-to-image translation, and efficient neural architectures for computer vision. I am author of peer-reviewed publications at international venues including WACV and CVPR Workshops, with recent work on state space models (Mamba) for style transfer and diffusion acceleration. I have a strong experience in PyTorch-based research pipelines, experimental evaluation, and efficiency/accuracy trade-offs.
